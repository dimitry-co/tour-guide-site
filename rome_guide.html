Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city with over 2,500 years of history. Explore the Colosseum, Vatican City, and vibrant streets filled with culture and cuisine.

Top Attractions

  • The Colosseum - Ancient amphitheater and a marvel of Roman engineering.
  • Vatican City - Home to St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel.
  • Trevi Fountain - A stunning baroque fountain where visitors toss coins for luck.
  • Roman Forum - Walk among the ruins of ancient government buildings.
