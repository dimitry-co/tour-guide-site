Japan, a country of traditions and innovation, offers breathtaking natural landscapes, iconic landmarks like Mount Fuji, and vibrant cityscapes filled with rich history and culture.

Top Attractions

  • Mount Fuji - Japan's iconic and majestic volcanic peak.
  • Tokyo - Explore the bustling city with attractions like Shibuya Crossing and the Tokyo Skytree.
  • Kyoto - Visit stunning temples such as Fushimi Inari Shrine and the Golden Pavilion.
  • Cherry Blossoms - Witness the breathtaking beauty of Sakura in full bloom.
